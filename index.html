About Us

The Lanigan Parent Teach Collaborative is a dedicated non-profit organization committed to fostering strong partnerships between parents, teachers, and the community. We work together to create enriching educational experiences and support our students in achieving their full potential.

Our Mission

To enhance the educational experience of all students by promoting active collaboration between families and educators, organizing meaningful events, and creating a supportive community that values learning and growth.
